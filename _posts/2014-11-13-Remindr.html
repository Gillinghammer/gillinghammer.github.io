Just a quick screenshot of the latest project I've begun developing. Remindr, is an app that helps you stay in touch with Friends, Family and Professional contacts. Eventually you'll receive a weekly email summary of those people you need to stay in touch with based on an interval you specify. The app is already taking shape and is functional after just 2 days of work. The app's stack is Angular on top of Rails and PostgreSQL.


I've also been using Bourbon.io and Neat from the great folks at ThoughtBot to move this project along more quickly.
