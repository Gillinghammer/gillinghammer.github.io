--- layout: post title: The Age of High Quality Video Promos ---

Ever since Kickstarter entered the scene we've seen countless examples of amazing promotional product videos. The production value of these videos is off the charts good.

I just came across a new group messaging service called 'Slack' and their video inspired me to write this short post. Head to Slack.com and watch their slick Slack video. Then tryout their product because I know most offices struggle with internal communications and organization of files.

In my opinion the guys over at DollarShaveClub.com really got this trend of funny high quality videos going when their 'Our Blades Are F***ing Great' video went viral on YouTube with over 16 million views.

About to launch your new app or product? Make it stand out and go viral with a great video!
